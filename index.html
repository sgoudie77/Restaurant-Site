Stone Grill History

Stone first opened its doors in 1986 and is modeled after famous BBQ restuarants in Texas. The lunch and dinner menu features grilled steaks, chops and seafood prepared in 8 different styles. Featured dishes are given a modern twist by our Head Chef, and our signature desserts are too delicious not to share.
